Population Map Application

Goal

There are four main purposes to the application:

  • Ability to view different basemaps as a backdrop.
  • Ability to view a population map that shows scaled symbols based on the population size.
  • Ability to navigate to 5 locations of your choice around the world.
  • Ability to click on a map symbol and view three attributes for that symbol.

To create this application,I use many of the concepts I have learned in class.
